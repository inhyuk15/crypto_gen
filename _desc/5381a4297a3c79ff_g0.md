## **1 Specification** 

SILC (which stands for SImple Lightweight CFB, and is pronounced as тАЬsilkтАЭ) is a blockcipher mode of operation for authenticated encryption with associated data (AEAD), which is also called an authenticated cipher. SILC is built upon CLOC [10,11,12], and the design of SILC aims at optimizing the hardware implementation cost of CLOC. SILC also maintains the provable security based on the pseudorandomness of the underlying blockcipher. SILC is suitable for use within constrained hardware devices.
