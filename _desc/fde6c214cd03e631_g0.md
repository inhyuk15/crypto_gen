## **2.2 Parameters** 

**Members of** Romulus **.** Romulus consists of Romulus-N, Romulus-M, Romulus-T and RomulusH. Romulus-N implements nonce-based AE (NAE) secure against Nonce-respecting adversaries, and Romulus-M implements nonce Misuse-resistant AE (MRAE) introduced by Rogaway and Shrimpton [69].
